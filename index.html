Summer season is the hottest season of the year. In this season the temperature
became so high that water starts to evaporate very quickly. ... Usually,
summers last for mid or later March to June but they can
expend up to the first week of July due to a delay of monsoon.



The Rainy Season, also known as the monsoon season begins in the middle of June
and continues up till early September. It comes after the end of the scorching summer
season. It's the time when most of the rainfall occurs; the sky is generally cloudy
throughout the season
